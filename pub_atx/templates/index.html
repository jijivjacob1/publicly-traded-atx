Exploring Austin's Top Publicly Traded Companies Through Data

This site gives you the opportunity to take an in-depth look at Austin's business environment. It details financial data of the city's top 31 publicly traded companies. Visitors are able to explore trading, revenue, market cap data, and much more. The purpose is to provide tools to explore and learn about the Austin economy through the lens of its largest companies.

The primary data source for this project was a company metadata from the Austin Business Journal. That information was supplemented with data from Quandl and IEX.


Many people have preconceived ideas about the Austin economy. Most see it as a high-tech mecca, much like Silicon Valley in California. In many ways, those assumptions are accurate. Tech giants such as Apple, IBM, and Google all have large offices in the Austin area. The city is also home to various tech starts including HomeAway and WP Engine.

However, Austin's economy has so much more to offer. The chart below gives you the opportunity to explore the city's public companies from three different angles. Each box represents one of the 31 companies with global headquarters in Austin. You can use the buttons below the chart to toggle views.

First, look at them scaled by market capitalization (total outstanding shares * market value per share). This will give you a view of which companies have the greatest overall value. You can also look at them scaled by the largest employers in the Austin market. This will show you the direct impact these companies have in our local economy as job creators. Lastly, look at them scaled by total employee base. This will give you a feel for the number of employees outside of Austin and the impact of these businesses on other economies.

Austin is a community that is known for its traffic. This map shows you the location of all the publicly traded companies. This is an opportunity to see if you see any clustering of business types. The hot spots are downtown Austin and the North Austin area known as The Domain.

Click on the markers on the map for more information about the company and a link to the company overview page.
